Privacy Policy

Cheat Meal Again respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

The app may access photos or the camera only when you choose to take or select a food photo. These images are used solely to estimate calorie information.

How Information Is Used

Food images and related data are processed to provide calorie estimation. No personal data is sold or shared for advertising.

Data Storage

Food records and settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses a cloud-based AI service to analyze food images.
